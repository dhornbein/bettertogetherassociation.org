Host Application

You are an expert and a resource to our community of independent workers. We invite you to share your expertise by participating in Better Together’s regularly scheduled program for the enrichment of our members. Our purpose is to improve the quality of life for independent workers by providing accessible, enriching, and engaging events–both virtual and in-person.


We are looking for hosts to develop regular scheduled workshops and events at least once a month starting with a 3-month commitment. The topic, format, and frequency are up to you.


Better Together will assist with logistics, production, and publishing. We are also invested in building your presentation skills and will provide you coaching or support in creating content.


You will own the content, we simply ask for a permissive license so that we can share it with our members.
